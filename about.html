Why "Fierce Whiskers" you ask? I have two cats. One we refer to as "fierce whiskers" and the other we refer to as "chubbers". Chubbers did not sound like a great choice of domain name for many reasons and anything I came up with using my name was already taken.
Two projects I completed during my Code Immersion program are a Blog app using HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and Ruby and a Movie Database, using HTML, CSS, and Angular. For the final project, my teammate and I created a single page app to more easily track finances and it is still in progress.
